# HealthcareWale Product Requirements

## Overview
Premium healthcare SaaS platform optimized for Indian healthcare workflows (Tier-2/Tier-3), pharmacy operations, and AI-driven clinic management.

## Design System: HealthcareWale
- **Style:** Premium modern SaaS, Stripe/Linear/Vercel inspired.
- **Colors:** Medical Teal, Deep Blue, Emerald Green, Amber Alerts.
- **Typography:** Inter, large readable labels, multilingual support (Hindi, Bengali).
- **Principles:** Low cognitive load, large touch targets, voice-first, WhatsApp-integrated.

## Screen List
1. **Premium Pharmacy Dashboard:** Medicine billing, inventory, expiry tracking, GST invoicing, AI reorder.
2. **Premium Clinic Dashboard:** Appointment scheduling, live patient queue, AI receptionist logs, WhatsApp automation.
3. **AI Receptionist Operations Center:** Live AI calls, transcripts, sentiment analysis, human takeover controls.
4. **Hospital HMS (Nursing Home optimized):** OPD/IPD, PMJAY claims, bed management, occupancy analytics.
5. **B2B Distributor Dashboard:** Retailer management, delivery tracking, territory heatmaps, demand forecasting.
6. **Founder Dashboard:** Super-admin view, revenue tracking, AI usage metrics, churn prediction.
