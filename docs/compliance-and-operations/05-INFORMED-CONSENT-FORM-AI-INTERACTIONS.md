## PART A: WHAT YOU ARE CONSENTING TO

By registering on HealthcareWale and using any feature of the platform (app, website, WhatsApp bot, or AI voice service), you confirm that you have read, understood, and agree to the following:

### A1. Nature of the AI System

HealthcareWale uses Artificial Intelligence (AI) technology to:

✅ **Help you book, reschedule, and cancel doctor appointments**  
✅ **Send you appointment reminders via WhatsApp, SMS, and phone call**  
✅ **Answer questions about doctor availability, clinic timings, and consultation fees**  
✅ **Manage your medicine delivery orders and prescription records**  
✅ **Help pharmacies and clinics manage their daily operations**

❌ **The AI does NOT and CANNOT:**  
- Diagnose any medical condition  
- Recommend treatments, medications, or dosages  
- Provide second opinions on your health condition  
- Assess the urgency or severity of your medical situation  
- Replace the professional judgment of a licensed doctor  

### A2. The AI Is Not Your Doctor

**You understand and accept that:**

1. The AI voice agent, WhatsApp bot, and all automated features of HealthcareWale are **administrative tools** — they are equivalent to a booking secretary, not a medical professional.

2. Any information you share with the AI (symptoms, health concerns, current medicines) is used ONLY to route you to the appropriate doctor specialty or to complete your booking. It is NOT analyzed, diagnosed, or acted upon clinically by the AI.

3. If you share symptoms like "I have chest pain" or "I can't breathe," the AI will immediately direct you to call **108 (Emergency Ambulance)** or visit the nearest emergency room. **The AI will not attempt to assess or manage medical emergencies.**

4. **No doctor-patient relationship is established between you and HealthcareWale or its AI system.** A doctor-patient relationship is established only when you consult a licensed medical practitioner through or outside the platform.

### A3. Teleconsultation (If Applicable)

If you use HealthcareWale's teleconsultation feature:
- Consultations are conducted by licensed, NMC/State Council-registered medical practitioners only
- The platform facilitates the connection — it does not participate in the clinical consultation
- The doctor's assessment, diagnosis, and prescription are entirely the doctor's professional responsibility
- Telemedicine has limitations — your doctor may ask you to visit in person if your condition requires physical examination
- Telemedicine consultations follow the **Telemedicine Practice Guidelines, 2020** issued by the Board of Governors in supersession of the Medical Council of India

## PART C: PLATFORM LIMITATIONS & EMERGENCY DISCLAIMER

### C1. Platform Is NOT an Emergency Service

**⚠️ CRITICAL: HealthcareWale is NOT an emergency medical service.**

- Do NOT use HealthcareWale to seek help in a medical emergency
- In any emergency — call **108 (National Ambulance)** or visit the nearest hospital emergency room immediately
- The platform does NOT have real-time emergency response capabilities
- Booking an appointment through HealthcareWale does NOT guarantee immediate medical attention

### C2. No Guarantee of Medical Outcomes

HealthcareWale facilitates connections between patients and licensed medical professionals. We do not control, supervise, or guarantee:
- The clinical quality of medical consultations conducted through the platform
- The accuracy of prescriptions issued by doctors through the platform
- The outcome of any medical treatment following a HealthcareWale-facilitated consultation
- The availability or quality of medicines delivered by partner pharmacies

### C3. PMJAY / Insurance Claims

HealthcareWale assists with PMJAY and Swasthya Sathi claim filing as a technology facilitator. We do not guarantee claim approval — final decisions rest with the NHA or state health department. Rejection of claims due to clinical documentation issues is the responsibility of the treating hospital, not HealthcareWale.
