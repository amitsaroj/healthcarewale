### 💵 1.1 SaaS Subscription Revenue (Your Biggest Income)

This is your core and most reliable income. Every clinic, pharmacy, hospital, and distributor that uses your platform pays you a monthly/annual fee. You collect this directly via Razorpay.

|---|---|---|---|
| Medical Store / Pharmacy (Basic) | Starter | ₹299 | ₹2,990 |
| Medical Store / Pharmacy (Pro) | Growth | ₹599 | ₹5,990 |
| Medical Store / Pharmacy (Advanced) | Enterprise | ₹1,499 | ₹14,990 |
| Clinic / Solo Doctor (Basic) | Starter | ₹499 | ₹4,990 |
| Clinic / Solo Doctor (Pro) | Growth | ₹999 | ₹9,990 |
| Clinic / Solo Doctor (Enterprise) | Pro Max | ₹2,999 | ₹29,990 |
| Nursing Home / Small Hospital | Basic HMS | ₹4,999 | ₹49,990 |

| Pharma Distributor | Basic | ₹2,999 | ₹29,990 |

**How money flows to you:**
```
Customer pays ₹999/month via Razorpay
→ Razorpay deducts 2% (₹20)
→ ₹979 lands in HealthcareWale bank account
→ After your server/API costs (~₹300/customer), you keep ₹679 net margin
→ At 1,000 customers → ₹6.79 Lakh NET profit/month
```

**Annual subscription discount:** Offer 2 months free on annual plans. This gives you 12 months of cash upfront which helps fund operations.

### 💵 1.2 AI Voice Call Revenue (Usage-Based Income)

Every AI phone call made on behalf of any clinic or pharmacy earns you money. This is charged on top of subscription.

| Call Type | Who Pays | Your Rate | Your Cost (Exotel + AI) | Your Margin |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Inbound patient call (AI answers) | Clinic | ₹2/minute | ₹0.80/minute | ₹1.20/min |
| Outbound reminder call | Clinic | ₹1.50/call | ₹0.60/call | ₹0.90/call |
| Supplier order call (AI places order) | Pharmacy | ₹3/call | ₹1.20/call | ₹1.80/call |
| PMJAY follow-up call | Hospital | ₹2/call | ₹0.80/call | ₹1.20/call |

**Example:** A clinic with 50 patients/day gets:
- 30 inbound AI calls × ₹2 × 2 min avg = ₹120/day
- 50 outbound reminders × ₹1.50 = ₹75/day
- **Total: ₹195/day = ₹5,850/month from that one clinic**
- At 500 clinics: ₹29.25 Lakh/month from voice alone

### 💵 1.3 WhatsApp Message Revenue (Usage-Based Income)

WhatsApp Business API charges per conversation. You buy at BSP rates and sell at markup.

| Message Type | Your Cost (BSP) | You Charge | Your Margin per conversation |
|---|---|---|---|
| Utility message (reminders, alerts) | ₹0.18 | ₹0.35 | ₹0.17 |
| Authentication (OTP) | ₹0.12 | ₹0.25 | ₹0.13 |
| Marketing message (campaigns) | ₹0.53 | ₹1.00 | ₹0.47 |
| Service message (free window) | ₹0 | ₹0 | — |

**Example:** 1,000 pharmacies sending 20 reminders/day:
- 1,000 × 20 × ₹0.35 = ₹7,000/day = ₹2.1 Lakh/month revenue
- Your cost: ₹1.08 Lakh/month
- **Net: ₹1.02 Lakh/month from WhatsApp billing**

## 2. PRICING STRATEGY

### 2.1 Pricing Philosophy
- **Penetration pricing** in Year 1: Price low enough to remove hesitation. ₹299/month for pharmacy is less than one expired strip of antibiotic.
- **Value-based pricing** in Year 2+: Raise prices by 30–50% once users are dependent.
- **Freemium-to-Paid**: Never truly free. Always have a 14-day free trial then charge.
- **Annual plan push**: Offer 2 months free for annual = get 12 months cash upfront.

**What HealthcareWale is NOT:**

## 7. RETENTION & UPSELL STRATEGY

### 7.1 Churn Prevention

**Warning signs of churning customer:**
- Login frequency drops below 3x/week
- Bills created drop by 50%+ from previous month
- No response to 2+ WhatsApp messages

**Churn prevention actions:**
- Automated n8n alert to you when these signals fire
- Personal WhatsApp message within 24 hours
- Offer: Free 1-on-1 training session
- If still at risk: Offer 1 month free extension
- Last resort: Downgrade to free plan (keeps them in ecosystem)

## 8. PARTNERSHIP & CHANNEL STRATEGY

### 8.1 Pharma MR (Medical Representative) Partner Program

**Structure:**
- MR refers a pharmacy/clinic → that customer pays for 30+ days → MR earns ₹300–500
- MR gets a unique referral code to track their conversions
- Monthly payout via UPI/bank transfer
- Top MRs (5+ referrals/month) get bonus ₹2,000 + recognition

**Why MRs will love this:**
- They already visit 40–60 pharmacies/week
- It's zero extra work — just mention HealthcareWale when visiting
- ₹300–500 per referral × 10 referrals/month = ₹3,000–5,000 extra income for them

**How to recruit MRs:**
- LinkedIn search: "Medical Representative Lucknow / Gorakhpur"
- Pharma company local offices
- WhatsApp groups for pharma professionals

### 8.2 IMA Chapter Partnership

- Approach IMA chapter secretary (not president — secretary is more accessible)
- Offer: Free 6-month access for all IMA member doctors in the chapter
- Ask in return: 5-minute slot at monthly chapter meeting + endorsement letter
- One IMA chapter endorsement = 50–300 doctors who trust you instantly
