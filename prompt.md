- Medical store and hospital inventory management via voice and app
- Supplier-to-store medicine supply chain management with automated ordering, billing, and communication
- Virtual waiting rooms and online queuing
- Follow-up scheduling and patient tracking
- Medical billing and coding automation
- Appointment reminders and notifications (fully automated)
- Vendor-to-medical-store/hospital automated requirement collection via calls, messages, and billing
- AI Voice Agents for healthcare industry management
- Patient support and enquiry handling
- Healthcare business automation workflows
- Patient follow-up and reminder automation
- Medical office management and operations support
- Healthcare customer service automation

The task was to analyze the platform from every possible angle and provide a brutally honest India-market-specific strategic assessment.
